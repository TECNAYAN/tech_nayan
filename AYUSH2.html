9 Most Famous Hindu Temples In Kanpur With Details

A large industrial city, Kanpur is constantly developing at a rapid pace in harmony with culture, tradition and modernization. Amidst all these developments, one thing that has remained the same is the faith and belief of people in the divine that can be witnessed at the temples in Kanpur, where people of all standard of life come together and pray like one under the same roof.

Beautiful Temples in Kanpur With Images:

1. Bramhakuti Temple:

Brahmakuti temple located in the city of Kanpur is the shrine of Lord Brahma, who is believed to be the supreme creator. The temple is located on the banks of Ganges River. This temple is the only Brahma temple in Kanpur area other than a small temple named Brahmeshwar Mahadeva. Thus, the temple is often visited by pilgrims all over the city.

  • Address: Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh
  • Timings: 9 am to 9 pm
  • Dress Code: No dress code
  • Approx. Visit Duration: 1-2 hours
  • How to Reach: Local modes of transport
  • Temple Website: N/A
  • Best Time to Visit: Bramhotsavam
