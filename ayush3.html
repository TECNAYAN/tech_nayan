2. Radha Krishnan Temple:

Radha Krishnan temple or JK Temple Kanpur houses the idols of Lord Krishna, Radha, Lord Narayan, Goddess Laxmi, Lord Hanuman, Lord Ardhanarishwar and Lord Narmadeshwar. The temple was constructed by the Singhania family in 1960. The temple’s calm environment offers peace of mind to those who are seeking for it.

  • Address: First Street, Sarvodaya Nagar, Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh 208005
  • Timings: 5 am to 12 pm and 4 pm to 10 pm
  • Dress Code: Decent Attire
  • Approx. Visit Duration: ½ hour to 1 hour
  • How to Reach: Local mode of transport
  • Temple Website: N/A
  • Best Time to Visit: Janmashtami, Holi
  • Other Attractions: Temples, small shops
